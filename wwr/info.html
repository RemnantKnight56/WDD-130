What is Dry Oar?

Dry Oar has been open since 2009, providing rafting opportunities to over 200,000 customers in our 13-year history. While business has been impacted due to closures in 2020 and 2021, we still strive to provide the best canoeing and rafting opportunities around!

We provide these services to any who come to Dry Oar for their rafting trip:

  • Round trip down the local river
  • One way down exciting white water rapids
  • Casual paddling in a lake
